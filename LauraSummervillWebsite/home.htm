Me and My Interests

Professional Profile

I am a Project Manager who specializes in database, LEAN solutions, and corporate building design.

I have a bachelors in Psychology, Associates in Computer Information Systems, certificates in SCRUM, Project Management, LEAN and Six Sigma.

I love working with teams and empowering them towards acheiving our goals.

Outdoor Activities

When I'm not working, I am a coxswain for a rowing team at Lake Union Crew in Seattle, WA.

For those of you who may not know what a coxswain is, it is the person who steers the boat and manages the team.

My current team competes both nationally and internationally and we love to take home GOLD!

Volunteer Work

I volunteer for the Reading with Rover program with my dog, Charlie.

We are a therapy team to help children improve their literacy skills in an encouraging environment.

We collaborate with the library system to connect with the community.
